Methodology

The AI Presence Score™ —
five dimensions.

A proprietary 100-point index developed by Appear AI. Measures exactly how visible, credible, and citable your business is across all three major AI platforms.

20
AI Mention Rate

How often you appear unprompted across 20 category queries. The most direct measure of AI awareness.

25
Citation Quality

When AI mentions you — how specific and credible is the description? Named credentials score far above generic language.

20
Competitor Dominance

How often do competitors appear in your target queries? Their dominance directly reduces your effective score.

20
Content Readability

How structured is your content for AI extraction? Schema markup, FAQ blocks, and llms.txt all factor here.

15
Entity Clarity

Does AI have a clear, consistent identity for your business? Conflicting signals across platforms reduce this sharply.
